Some playing suggestions:

  1. The chords in the beginning of the verses can be expanded to follow the melody:
    Ebmaj7 . . Eb6 | Dm7 . Eaug E
  2. The record has the chromatic descent beginning at ‘I’m always …’ played both in the bass line and in a higher register. It is probably best to choose one of them; see chord suggestions below.
  3. The chords to that descent could be conceived of (and played) either as notated below, or as Am Ammaj7 C/g D9/f#.
