Written out with a capo on the 1st fret (sounding key: Bb). Another alternative is to use a 3rd fret capo and the chords G, Em, Am, D11, which is easier to play, but misses the deep, full E sound for the last chord in each ‘round’.

Lots of things going on here, even though it sounds simple enough. Especially the chord noted as an E11 is only vaguely defined. Alternatives to E11 are plain E, E7, or some other muddy version of E.
