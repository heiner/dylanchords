Dixie

Trad.
Played by Bob Dylan and band in Masked and Anonymous (2003)
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

Someone, probably Dylan, plays the F (or something like it) in the refrain straight through the following D, and embellishes the following G with a C/g-G figure. The second time, the F is played right through the G as well. Economy of means, I think it's called...
The Csus4 in the last line of the refrain could just as well be played as an F (or for that matter, as a G – works just as well).
