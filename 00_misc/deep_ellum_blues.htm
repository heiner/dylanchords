Deep Ellem Blues

Written by Joe & Bob Attlesay (but credited to Big Joe Williams by Dylan)
Performed live by Bob Dylan on April 16, 1962 at Gerde's Folk City
Tabbed by Eyolf Østrem

The track begins with a lot of tuning; the guitar has been in open E tuning before the song begins...
In the first verse, Dylan reverses the basic strumming pattern (G6 - G - G6 - G etc.)
