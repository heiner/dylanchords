God Knows

Words and music Bob Dylan
Recorded – and discarded – for Oh Mercy, but given a second chance on Under The Red Sky (1990).
May not have come out perfectly in the studio, but live this is a gem.

I give two versions: one with G chords, the other with E chords. Both seem to have been used, but what he plays in concerts it is actually the E version, although the intro (before the band enters) sounds suspiciously like his dear old G-C/g-G turn.
