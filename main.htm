Reminder #1: There's more to this site than just the chords. Check out the professors section, with the articles about Dylan and his music. Most of it is collected and nicely typeset (if I may say so) in a pdf book, but I have also prepared html versions of the individual articles.
I can recommend the chapters about the guitar playing on Don't Think Twice, about the “mathematical music” in Chronicles, and the discussion about plagiarism on Modern Times and the previous albums. And if you still have time, or want a general overview over Dylan's musical carreer, that's what The Uneven Heart is meant to give.

June 14: The zip file has been updated, and so has Seal, which should now work well even on Windows.

What you are looking at is an unofficial mirror of Eyolf Østrem's dylanchords.com, much of which is temporarily unavailable due to the American Music Publishers' Association (MPA)'s "rattling of sabres against tab sites". Thankfully I have been able to track down a recent copy of the site, which I have uploaded as a service to fellow guitar-playing Dylan fans who may be experiencing withdrawal symptoms.

This mirror is also available as a ZIP file which you are encouraged to download in case this mirror is forced offline.

Please see the Mail page if you would like to contact Eyolf Østrem, the creator of the original Dylanchords site.
